Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking?

To make a booking, simply navigate to the "Book Now!" page and follow the prompts to select your desired date, time, and service. Then, fill out the required information and confirm your booking.

Can I print the Booking Receipt and Order Receipt?

Yes absolutely! Just click the Print Button displayed on the page.

How can I add the products to Cart

At the shopping page, just click at the add to cart button and it will shown at the Cart Icon that your products have been added.

How to checkout order?

At the cart page, click the checkout button and you will be redirected to the checkout page.
